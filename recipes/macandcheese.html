Cauliflower Mac-N-Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup Cauliflower florets
  • cooking spray
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cups shredded chedder cheese
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 (8 ounces) package cream cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain.

  2. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer. Bring water to a boil. Add Cauliflower, cover, and steam until tender, 5 to 6 minutes. Place Cauliflower in a blender or food processor; blend or food processor; blend until smooth.

  3. Coat a large saucepan with non-stick cooking spray; place over medium heat. Add oil and flour; cook, stirring constantly, until a thick paste forms, 1 to 2 minutes. Add milk; cook and stir until mixture thickens, 3 to 4 minutes.

  4. Mix Cauliflower, chedder cheese, cream cheese, salt, and pepper into milk mixture; stir until sauce is smooth. Fold macaroni into sauce.
